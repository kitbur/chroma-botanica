About Me

I am Maurice Pillard Verneuil, a French artist and designer. My artistic journey began under the mentorship of the renowned Swiss designer Eugène Grasset. This foundational experience shaped my development as I became a prominent figure in the Art Nouveau and later, the Art Deco movements.
